About

Angelo Corsaro is a globally recognized expert in distributed computing and the inventor of the Zenoh Protocol. With decades of experience designing high-performance middleware and communication protocols, he has shaped the way modern distributed systems communicate at scale.

His work spans real-time systems, edge computing, IoT, and robotics — domains where efficiency, scalability, and low latency are not optional but essential. Angelo is a frequent keynote speaker, open-source advocate, and thought leader in the distributed systems community.

Building open technologies that empower developers and organizations.
