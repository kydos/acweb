Queryables & Replies

Queryables are Zenoh's abstraction for data at rest and remote computation. They generalise both storage lookups and RPC under a single, location-transparent API.

Key Insight

Queryables blur the line between "get from storage" and "compute on demand". A queryable can:

  • Return a cached value (acting as a storage)
  • Compute a result on the fly (acting as a service)
  • Fan out to other Zenoh resources and aggregate results
